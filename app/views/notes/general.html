Warrington research
Date of design
March 2019
Designers
Becky Lee and Adele Murray

Research notes

  • Agents felt they were able to handle many of the common enquiries
  • The redesigned layout, with the more prominent status and separate claim history, worked well
  • All agents were able to suspend payments for someone going into prison
  • Some agents were unsure what the ‘overview’ tab contains
  • Need to explore the need for a more comprehensive payment history

Scenarios

  • Citizen calls up to ask why their payments have stopped
  • Agent needs to suspend payments because someone went into prison
  • Changing marital details
  • When is my next payment and when was my last payment?
