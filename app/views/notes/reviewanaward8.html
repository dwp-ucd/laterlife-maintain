User research scenarios

  1. SRB received
    Agent checks OLE and identifies that the increase in SP was due to a late paid contribution
    The late paid contribution was received by HMRC on xx/xx/xxxx
    Agent changes the review date and continues
  2. SRB received
    Agent checks OLE and identifies that the increase in SP was due to a late paid contribution
    The late paid contribution was received by HMRC on xx/xx/xxxx (different uprating year as SPa)
    Agent changes the review date and amount and continues
  3. SRB received
    Agent checks OLE and identifies that the increase in SP was due to 2 late paid contributions The first late paid contribution was received by HMRC on xx/xx/xxxx
    The agent changes the amount displayed and enters date for the first late paid contribution The second late paid contribution was received by HMRC on xx/xx/xxxx
    The agent adds another review and add/change the amount and date for the second late paid contribution Agent continues
