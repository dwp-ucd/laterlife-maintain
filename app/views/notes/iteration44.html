Scenario

  • Agent can record the from, to and payment date of a manual payment. The amount is then calculated based on those dates and saved in the payment history. The next payment date is updated based on the manual payment recorded.

Business requirements

  • Agent needs to be able to enter the following details of a payment made manually through CPS local: payment period, payment amount, payment date

Related stories

  • SA-3154 - As an agent I want to record when I have made a manual payment in CPS local so that overpayments do not occur
