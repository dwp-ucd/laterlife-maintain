Recalling payments

not tested
Date of design
July 2019
Designers
Dinesh Jayachandran and Colin Jones

Scenario

  • Agent takes a change of bank account from a citizen and is prompted to recall the payment that is being sent to the old account
  • If an agent takes a change of bank account, they need to have the option to try and recall a payment that is being sent to the old account

Business requirement

  • If an agent knows a payment is going to an invalid bank account, they should try and recall it rather than waiting for it to come back from the bank
