Delay your start date

Your State Pension will increase by just over 0.1% for every week that you delay your start date. As long as you delay by at least 9 weeks.

You may need to delay longer than 9 weeks if you've been:

  • in prison
  • on certain benefits (opens in a new tab)

It could take around 18 years to get back the money you delay taking.
