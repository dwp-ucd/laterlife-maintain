When do you want to calculate your State Pension from?

13 October 2021 is the earliest date you can choose.

13 February 2023 is the latest date you can start from (if you claim today).

If you choose 22 November 2021 or earlier you will not get any extra State Pension.

It may take around 18 years to get back any money you delay taking.
