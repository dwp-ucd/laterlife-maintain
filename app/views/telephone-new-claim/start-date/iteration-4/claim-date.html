What date do you want your State Pension to be calculated from?

You can only claim your State Pension 4 months in advance.

URL, errors and notes
URL: /state-pension-claim-date
Errors: Nothing entered: Enter the date the claimant wants their State Pension to be calculated from
Invalid: Enter a real date, like 12 4 1993
(Late claims) If user puts in date after SPA date BUT more than 12 months ago: Date cannot be more than 12 months before today
(Any claim) If user puts in date before SPA date: Date cannot be before claimant's State Pension date
