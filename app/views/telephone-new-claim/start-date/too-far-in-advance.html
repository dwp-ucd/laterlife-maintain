The claim date is too far in advance

Explain to the citizen that their State Pension cannot start more than 4 months after the day they put in their claim.

The citizen can either:

  • select a new date which is within the next 4 months
  • call back four months before the date they want their claim to start

If the citizen asks, you can tell them they may get more money if they put off claiming their State Pension into the future (opens in a new tab).
